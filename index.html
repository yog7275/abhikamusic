About the Artist

Abhi

Lead Vocalist & Visionary

Where Emotion Meets Rhythm

Abhi Ka Music @ Deep Heart is not just a band; it's a soulful musical experience. Spearheaded by Abhi, a singer whose voice is guided by emotions, love, and heart-to-heart connections. We believe that music is the ultimate expression of unconditional love, connecting humans beyond boundaries.

With over a decade of live performance experience, our journey spans hundreds of stages, from grand corporate celebrations and romantic destination weddings to electrifying college fests and intimate musical nights. Our music touches the core of the listener, bringing a wave of nostalgia, excitement, and deep joy.
